Partnerships in Action

This gallery presents real-world examples of collaboration that reflect the principles of Sustainable Development Goal 17. The visuals highlight how governments, international organizations, private companies, and communities work together to achieve sustainable outcomes.

Each image represents a different form of partnership, including global policy dialogue, technological innovation, financing initiatives, and cross-sector cooperation.

All images are sourced from official United Nations platforms and partner organizations, representing authentic examples of multi-stakeholder partnerships contributing to the 2030 Agenda.

Types of Partnerships Featured

Sustainable Development Goal 17 emphasizes collaboration across sectors and borders. The partnerships showcased in this gallery represent key models used to advance the 2030 Agenda.

  • Public–private partnerships supporting sustainable financing
  • International cooperation led by the United Nations
  • Technology and innovation partnerships for data and AI solutions
  • Multi-stakeholder collaborations involving civil society
  • Capacity-building initiatives in developing countries
