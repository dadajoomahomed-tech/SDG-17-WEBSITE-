About This Project

This website was developed as part of the Web Development (CDMU 2413) course at the University of Cyberjaya. The project focuses on Sustainable Development Goal 17: Partnerships for the Goals, highlighting the importance of collaboration in achieving global sustainability.

Project Purpose

The primary aim of this project is to promote awareness of SDG 17 and demonstrate how partnerships across governments, private sectors, civil society, and academic institutions are essential to achieving the United Nations 2030 Agenda.

By combining informative content with visual design and interactive elements, this website encourages users to understand, explore, and participate in collaborative efforts for sustainable development.

Key Focus Areas

  • Encouraging multi-stakeholder collaboration at local and global levels
  • Supporting financial and resource mobilization for developing countries
  • Promoting technology transfer and innovation sharing
  • Strengthening capacity building and knowledge exchange
  • Improving global policy coordination for sustainable development
  • Enhancing transparency, monitoring, and accountability mechanisms

Design & Development Approach

The website was designed using fundamental web development principles, including semantic HTML, responsive layouts, accessibility considerations, and user-centered design. Visual hierarchy and imagery were carefully selected to support content clarity and engagement.

The design reflects the values of SDG 17 by emphasizing connection, cooperation, and global unity through consistent layout and imagery.

Collaboration in Practice

Effective partnerships require shared goals, trust, and coordinated action. This project highlights how diverse stakeholders come together to align resources and expertise in support of the Sustainable Development Goals.

Through collaboration, organizations can amplify impact, reduce duplication of efforts, and accelerate progress toward global sustainability.
